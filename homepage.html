WELL LOG DATA VIEWER

  • About Well Log Viewer

    This is a web-based Well Log Viewer project which was done under ONGC. Oil and Natural Gas Corporation is an Indian government-owned crude oil and natural gas corporation. It is under the ownership of the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas, Government of India. Through this utility, we can visualize LAS files and get a graphical representation of the same using technologies like Python, Flask, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. We also get distinct information about the well and the graphs plotted. Well Log Viewer is an online web-based utility to quickly visualize and interpret well log data. User can upload a LAS file to this utility and visualize logs in different tracks/graphs. At present a maximum of 5 logs can be plotted in a single track.
